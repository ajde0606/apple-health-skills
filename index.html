Apple Health Bridge

A private, secure bridge that syncs your Apple Health data from iPhone to Mac and adds live Wahoo heart-rate capture for local analytics and proactive insights. No shared cloud backend, no extra accounts.

Learn More Get Support Privacy Policy

Highlights

Apple Health Bridge helps you keep control of your health data while making it more useful. It connects HealthKit data on your iPhone to your personal Mac collector over a secure Tailscale network, then enables local AI analysis for trends, alerts, and insights.

Key Benefits

  • Private by design: Data stays on your own devices.
  • Secure transport: Encrypted connection over Tailscale + HTTPS.
  • Fast setup: Connect with QR code and bootstrap recent history.
  • Background updates: Supports HealthKit observer delivery.
  • Live HR streaming: Ingest real-time Wahoo heart-rate data on Mac.
  • Actionable insights: Pair with your local AI workflow for alerts.

Product Overveiw

Apple Health Bridge turns your iPhone and Mac into a private health intelligence system. Sync heart rate, blood glucose, sleep, and more from Apple Health to your own Mac — then analyze patterns locally without relying on third-party cloud services.

You can also capture live heart-rate streams from compatible Wahoo sensors on Mac to complement Apple Health history with real-time workout monitoring.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026

Apple Health Bridge is built with a local-first privacy model. We do not sell personal data and we do not operate a centralized cloud database for your health records.

1. Data We Access

With your explicit permission, the app reads selected Apple HealthKit data types (for example heart rate, blood glucose, and sleep metrics) to sync them to your Mac collector.

2. How Data Is Used

  • To sync your health records from iPhone to your personal Mac collector.
  • To support local analysis, trend review, and optional alert workflows.
  • To provide app functionality you initiate (manual or background sync).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share health records with third-party advertisers. Data may be transmitted through your configured network tunnel (such as Tailscale) between your own devices.

4. Data Retention and Control

Data retention is controlled by your Mac collector configuration. You can delete historical data locally at any time and revoke Health permissions in iOS Settings.

5. Security

The app is designed to use encrypted connections and token-based authentication for ingestion endpoints. You are responsible for keeping your network and devices secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Apple Health Bridge is not directed to children under 13. If you believe personal data from a child was provided in error, contact us to request deletion guidance.

7. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to hornliquant@gmail.com.
